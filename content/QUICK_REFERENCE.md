## ðŸš¨ Red-Flag Examples (Include in Plans)

**Headaches:**
- Sudden, severe "thunderclap" headache
- Vision, speech, or balance changes
- Headache after injury or with fever/stiff neck

**Digestive:**
- Persistent pain or vomiting
- Blood in stool or black, tarry stool
- Rapid weight loss or severe fatigue

**Sleep:**
- Signs of sleep apnea (choking, gasping)
- Mood changes, depression, or anxiety spikes
- Sleep loss over 3 weeks impacting daily life
